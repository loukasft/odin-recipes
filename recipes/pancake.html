Description

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup mninced onion
  • 1 can crusa=hed tomatoes
  • 1 cup water
  • 4 tablespoons choped parsley
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 12 lasgna noodles
  • 1 large egg
  • 100 grams mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 tablespoon ground black

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Cook sausage,ground beef and onion over medium heat until well browned.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes,tomato sause and water.Simmer, covered for about 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  4. Cook lasagna noodels in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain the noodles and rinse with cold water.
  5. Assemble . Spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom ,arrange 6 noodles on top and spread the cheese
